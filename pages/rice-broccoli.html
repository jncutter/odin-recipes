Brown Rice and Broccoli

Steamed brown rice mixed with sautéed broccoli. This is a great side dish, and I honestly enjoy it more than my main dishes.

Ingredients

  • Brown rice
  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Measure out brown rice into a pot, 1/4 cup is a single serving
  2. Put double that volume in water into the pot (if you used 1/4 cup of rice then use 1/2 cup of water)
  3. Season the water with salt, 1/8 tsp of salt per 1/4 cup of rice
  4. Heat pot on high heat until water begins to boil
  5. Turn heat to low, and cook for 45 minutes
  6. While rice is cooking prep and cook the broccoli
  7. Use a food processor to blend the broccoli to small chunks, or cut it into small pieces (include stalk)
  8. 1 cup of broccoli chunks is a serving, if there are any leftovers they can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days
  9. Heat pan to medium high heat with some olive oil
  10. Put the broccoli in the heated pan and season with salt and pepper
  11. Sauté the broccoli, stir continuously
  12. This should only take a couple of minutes, as it cooks the broccoli will turn a darker green and start to brown
  13. When the broccoli is cooked to your liking take it off of the heat
  14. Mix together the rice and broccoli
