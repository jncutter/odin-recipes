Chicken Breast

Pan seared chicken breast. This is simple to make with room for your own alterations. Also, I don't measure anything. Sorry.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Any seasoning blend
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat pan to medium-high heat
  2. While pan is preheating season chicken breast
  3. Put some olive oil in pan when it is heated
  4. Cook chicken in pan
  5. Flip chicken after ~5 minutes
  6. Time will vary based on the size of the chicken. It is done when the internal temperature is 165Â°F
  7. When it is cooked through take the chicken out of the pan and let it rest.
